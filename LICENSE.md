## User Policy Commitment

Leximor is built on a principle of user good faith. The following policy
is a permanent commitment, documented here as public record:

When a paid subscription lapses, user accounts transition to dormant status
(Xim Remnant). No data is deleted. No charges are incurred without
explicit manual action by the user. Leximor will never auto-charge
a lapsed account.
